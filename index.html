Welcome to the Division

Founded in 1972, we are now the Computer Science Division within the Department of Mathematical Sciences. We bring together theory and practice — from Automata to Robotics, from NLP to Machine Learning.

Our 11 full-time academic staff and vibrant student community make this one of South Africa's leading CS departments.
